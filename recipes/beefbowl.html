Korean Beef Bowl Recipe

Quick and easy Korean Beef Bowl.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Soy sauce
  • Brown sugar
  • Scallions
  • garlic
  • Rice

Steps

  1. Cook the Rice
  2. Brown beef in pan until cooked through
  3. Mix soy sauce, sugar, etc. and pour over the beef
  4. Cook until most liquid is gone
  5. Done!

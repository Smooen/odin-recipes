Dumpling Recipe

Some SUCCULENT, hot dumplings

This recipe will show you how even an absolute shitter like you, can make some beautiful dumplings.

Ingredients:

  • Storebought, frozen dumpling wrappers
  • 500g of ground pork
  • Fuckton of scallions
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1nch knob of ginger
  • 250g - 500g of napa cabbage
  • Pantry spices

Steps:

  1. Make filling
  2. Stuff filling in wrappers
  3. Steam dumplings for about 8 minutes
  4. Make dipping sauce whilst dumpling are steaming
